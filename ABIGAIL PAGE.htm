ABIGAIL'S HERITAGE

By Char Jarman

In this historical novel, which was published in 2001, and is a sequel to "Maggie", the story of

Peever continues. Fictional characters are woven into the plot that relates some

of the actual events that happened in early Peever. Flood, fire,

bank robbery, and murder are retold in story form.

The time covers the years from early 1900's to

present day when Abigail Preston, now

Mrs. Larry MacRae,

moves to South Dakota with her husband and children and discovers an old family secret.

Rosemarie Hanssen has done the cover art..... note the skillful

manner in which she drew the ghostly image of the

children's ancestor Angelina Roulliard with the

Coteau Des Prairies in the background.

Rosemarie also did pencil

sketches throughout

the book.
