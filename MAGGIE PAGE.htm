MAGGIE..WOMAN OF ROBERTS COUNTY

By Char Jarman

This historical novel was published by Pinehill Press of Freeman, SD for Char Jarman in 1999. The setting is Roberts County, spanning the years of 1884 until the present. Char has incorporated some of the actual history of the area in retelling the stories of the county seat battles that waged until the town of Sisseton finally won the coveted prize.

Maggie Sinclair is a society girl who was raised in Chicago and as she is tired of her life as such, she answers an ad in the paper for a teacher. This brings her to Dakota Territory where she learns a new way of living.

Eventually, changes in her life bring her to the Peever area to teach among the Indian children at the Agency. Then later to the town of Peever itself to live with her husband and family.

Rosemarie Hanssen of rural Peever has done the beautiful cover in watercolors. It shows a view of early Peever from the hill east of town with the Coteau Des Prairies in the background.
