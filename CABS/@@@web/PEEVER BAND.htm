PEEVER BAND

DURING THE EARLY YEARS OF PEEVER THE NEWS IN THE PEEVER PILOT WOULD OCCASIONALLY HAVE A NOTE ABOUT BAND PRACTICE AND CONCERTS. THIS PICTURE WAS FOUND BY LENORE JOHNSTON OF SISSETON WHO IS A DAUGHTER OF HARRISON MURRAY. HARRISON IS PICTURED IN THE FRONT ROW BESIDE THE DRUM. HER UNCLE ALEC MURRAY IS STANDING IN THE MIDDLE OF THE BACK ROW.
