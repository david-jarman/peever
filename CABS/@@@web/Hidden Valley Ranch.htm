HIDDEN VALLEY RANCH

Co-owner of the ranch, W.W. (Doc) Miller (left), watches Patrick Pemrich of Summit, SD cook a meal for hungry picnickers at right. The latter, all of whom are from Peever, SD or Waubay, SD include: William Haubris, Jr., 15; Michael Haubris, 12; Pat Haubris, 8; Byron Gleason; Mrs. Ella Lake; Mrs. Clarice Fredrickson; Charles J. Tobin, co-owner of the ranch and William Haubris, Sr.

At the entrance to Hidden Valley Ranch, Miller gives Michael Haubris,12, of Peever some pointers on how to round up horses along the bank of one of the ranch's many streams.

Swinging over a creek at the ranch are Michael and Pat Haubris. In background: William Haubris, Jr.
